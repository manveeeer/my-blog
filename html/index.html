about

19. pursuing bachelors in accounts and finance, cfa from sd college, chandigarh. wanted to go to srcc instead.

december 6, 2024 — interviewed at an accelerator[1], got selected. started january 2nd as investment analyst, looking after a fund that backs early stage startups[2]. i play my little role there.

after six months, began building investor.to[3]. what i thought would be one of my biggest regrets turned out to be one of my biggest achievements.

class 12th scores: 98 in accountancy, topped in economics and business studies. flexing? maybe. earned it? definitely.

i like to read everything — essays, reports, research, publications, books. mostly non-fiction. occasionally anna huang type stuff.

virat is the best cricketer to exist. coldplay is the best band to exist. these are facts, not opinions.

if you've cared enough to read this far, go check out some essays i've put my soul into. or leave a comment telling me how great my writing is. why not?

why this page looks like this

most sites are designed to impress. this one is designed to be read.

no tracking. no analytics. no newsletter pop-ups. no dark patterns. just words and ideas.

the web used to be simple. text, links, thoughts. before everything became optimized for engagement and conversion.

this is a return to that. a place for thinking, not performing.
